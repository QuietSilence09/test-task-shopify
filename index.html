Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number by email. You can use this tracking number to track the progress of your shipment online.

What is delivery time of my order?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and chosen shipping method. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping can deliver within 1-2 business days.

How can I return my product?

You can return your product within 30 days of receipt. Simply visit our returns portal, enter your order number, and follow the instructions to generate a return label.

What are the shipping costs my order?

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and the weight of your order. Free shipping is available for orders over $50 within the continental US.

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, please contact our customer support team for assistance.
